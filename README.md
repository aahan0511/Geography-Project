### 1. Developing Human Capital

**Strategic interventions for population quality enhancement.**
_Ministry of Human Resource Development_

### 01. Education Policy

- **Universal Access:** Guaranteeing quality schooling for every child, regardless of geography or status.
- **Holistic Curriculum:** Moving beyond rote learning to critical thinking and life skills.
- **Teacher Training:** Empowering educators with modern pedagogical tools and continuous professional development.
- **Vocational Training:** Bridging the gap between academia and industry requirements.

### 02. Healthcare Infrastructure

_"A healthy individual is a productive asset to the nation."_

- **Immunization:** 100% coverage target.
- **Infant Mortality:** Drastic reduction in IMR.
- **Ayushman Bharat:** Focusing on preventive healthcare, mental well-being, maternal health, and affordable medical access for all citizens through comprehensive insurance coverage.

### 03. Skill Development

- **Technical Proficiency:** Equipping youth with industry-relevant technical expertise.
- **Entrepreneurship:** Fostering a culture of innovation and job creation.
- **Industry 4.0:** Preparing workforce for AI, Robotics, IoT, and emerging technologies.
- **Apprenticeships:** Hands-on learning through practical on-the-job training programs.

### 04. Digital India

- **E-Learning:** Revolutionizing education through digital classrooms and online resources.
- **Digital Literacy:** Ensuring every citizen can navigate the modern digital economy.
- **Broadband Connectivity:** Bringing high-speed internet to every village, bridging the digital divide.
- **Digital Payments:** Promoting cashless economy through UPI and mobile banking infrastructure.

### 05. Women Empowerment

_"Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao"_ - Educating the girl child is educating the entire family.

- **Key Initiatives:**
  - STEM Education promotion
  - Self Help Groups support
  - Safety & Security enhancement

### 06. Sports & Youth

Building world-class sports infrastructure, identifying grassroot talent, and creating pathways for athletes to achieve excellence on the global stage.

- **Fit India Movement**
- **Khelo India Games**
- **Target Olympic Podium Scheme (TOPS)**

### Our Vision

**Turning 1.4 Billion People into 1.4 Billion Opportunities.**
